# LMS - Online text

To submit online text, students complete the following steps:

**Step 1:** Click the ‘Add submission’ button to bring up the online text editor page

**Step 2:** Type the relevant text into a text editor, or paste from a previously written file.

**Step 3:** Click **‘Save Changes’**.

**Step 4:** There should now be a Last modified date and the first 100 characters entered will also be displayed.
